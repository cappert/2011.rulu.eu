Conference Details


Ruby Lugdunum 2011 is a two day, single track Ruby conference in Lyon, France.

The ruby ecosystem is thrilling in Europe with major events happening everywhere. Barricaded in the last free village in Gaul, we will treat you with talks (in English), some hacking and good food. Check out the schedule ! There will be plenty of time built in to meet the other attendees.

Thanks to our sponsors for helping to make this conference possible.
